Rationality; From AI to Zombies as on demand book

@2020/08/27

You can use lulu.com to print custom books. There is a project rationality-ai-zombies which formats the forementioned book into printable PDF. The cost of printing and postage is something like 20€.

It is worth mentioning, that the book is pretty dry and it may be better to try audiobook version instead. For example, I've read maybe one fifth of the book and listened to the rest.

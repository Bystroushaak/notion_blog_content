Interesting graffiti I've seen

@2020/09/12

Loose translation; "There is an endless number of possibilities", or maybe more word-to-word translation "Possibilities are of inexhaustible quantity".

I've been thinking about this one countless times in my life. There was an endless number of things that could be on this bulletin board. And yet, there was this.

Sometimes we act like our lives are a train on track that is moving in some expected direction, yet our lives are really full of endless possibilities. Just to realize this opens new doors.

Roughly translated:

I roam the moon,
or through the sky,
it can be quite haunting,
we are helpless to change it.

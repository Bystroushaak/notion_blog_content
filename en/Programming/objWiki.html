objWiki

objWiki is my take on the topic of personal wiki, personal information managers, and several other weird concepts relevant to work with information.

At the moment, the project is still in pre-alpha version, not available to the public. But I try to share at least stories from the development, screenshots and other relevant information.

When it gets to the point that it may be used by others, I'll release it here.

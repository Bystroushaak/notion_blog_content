Weekly updates

Regular updates, eh? Inspired by the post Staying Motivated by Sending Status Updates to Nobody and by Eevee's dev log, I've decided to try to write regular updates, or "newsletters", where I'll put together what I've done, experienced and generally survived.

I am not sure how much this will be interesting to other people, as this should be a tool for myself, to remember what I've done.
